Face with Tears of Joy

A yellow face with a big grin, uplifted eyebrows, and smiling eyes, each shedding a tear from laughing so hard. Widely used to show something is funny or pleasing.

🍻

Clinking Beer Mugs

Two frosty mugs of beer with frothy heads being clinked together, as done at a celebratory or convivial toast ("Cheers!"). Several platforms include stylized lines to mark the clinking.

🌱

Seedling

A seedling, or a young plant, as a newly sprouted tree. Depicted as a tiny, light-green plant with a short stem and two leaves. Often shown freshly sprung from a small mound of brown earth.

🌈

Rainbow

A multicolored arc of light in the sky, caused by the refraction and dispersion of sunlight by droplets of moisture in the Earth's atmosphere. Often associated with hope, happiness, and optimism.
